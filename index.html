Electronic Throttle Control System

Our electronic throttle valves work in conjunction with the engine control unit (ECU) to precisely regulate the airflow into the engine, ensuring optimal combustion under all operating conditions.

1

Sensor Input

The accelerator pedal position sensor sends signals to the ECU, indicating the driver's intent.

2

ECU Processing

The ECU processes input from multiple sensors and calculates the optimal throttle position.

3

Throttle Actuation

The electric motor adjusts the throttle valve position to achieve the desired airflow.

4

Feedback Loop

Position sensors provide real-time feedback to ensure precise control and immediate response.
